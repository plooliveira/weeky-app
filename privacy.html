Privacy Policy

Weeky is currently in active development. This policy describes how the app handles information in its current version.

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Your planning data stays local

Weeky stores your planning data locally on your device. Your weekly plans are not sent to a Weeky server.

No analytics

Weeky does not currently use analytics or tracking tools inside the application.

No account required

Weeky does not currently require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the application.

Changes to this policy

This policy will be updated if the way Weeky handles data changes. The latest version will remain available on this page.
